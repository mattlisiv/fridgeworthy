We improve students' academic performance

by showing that

hard work pays off.

At FridgeWorthy, our goal is to improve education by integrating rewards for students, teachers, and parents. We know sometimes students need motivation to keep their grades a top priority when many other activites are going on in their lives. We are here to remove the pressure from students, teachers, and parents. We want to make learning a fun, rewarding, and collaborative effort that engages the entire community. With our proud partners, we present exciting rewards that can be redeemed by FridgeWorthy members.

for the students...

Students earn points based on their quality of grades. These rewards can be redeemed for deals and items from businesses and organizations.


for the teachers...

Teachers can track students grades and accumulate their own points to use toward rewards.


for the parents...

Parents can get involved with their child's progress and receive notifications and status updates.


for the businesses...

Businesses, small and large, can gain exposure and customers from contributing to furthering future education.
